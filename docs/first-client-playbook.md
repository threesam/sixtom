# First-Client Playbook (AI Enablement + CRO)

## 1) Start with one exact niche

Use this positioning line everywhere (site, LinkedIn headline, outreach):

> I help ecommerce and content-led brands build practical AI workflows that improve conversion and reduce execution bottlenecks in 14-30 days.

Initial niche priority:

1. DTC ecommerce teams (fashion, cookware, lifestyle) with active online revenue
2. Content-heavy brands or media teams with recurring publishing needs

Do not target everyone. Pick one segment for 30 days, then review results.

## 2) Sell one offer first

Offer name: **14-Day AI Workflow Pilot**

- **Who it's for:** teams already shipping but stuck in tool chaos
- **Deliverables:** one production-ready workflow + KPI baseline + rollout doc
- **Typical outcomes:** faster content operations, cleaner merchandising workflows, fewer handoff delays
- **CTA:** "Book a niche-fit call"

## 3) First 30-day client pipeline

Weekly targets:

- 20 outbound messages to ideal buyers
- 2 live calls booked
- 1 written teardown/case-style audit shared publicly

Outreach structure:

1. Problem mirror ("Looks like you're handling X while trying to improve Y")
2. Observation ("I noticed friction in...")
3. Low-friction ask ("Open to a 20-minute fit call?")

## 4) LinkedIn growth system (simple + consistent)

Post 3x per week:

- **Post 1: POV** — your angle on AI enablement + CRO
- **Post 2: Proof** — teardown, mini-case, or before/after process insight
- **Post 3: Process** — how you build systems teams can actually adopt

Comment daily on 10 ICP posts (operators, founders, ecommerce leads).  
Prioritize useful comments over broad audience content.

## 5) Weekly review scorecard

Track each Friday:

- Profile views
- Inbound DMs
- Outbound sent
- Calls booked
- Proposals sent
- New client wins

Keep the niche fixed for at least 4 weeks before making major changes.
